    Student Toolkit

    Real advice for students, from a student

    - Using a planner to track assignments and deadlines can make sure you keep organized.

    - Always get started on tasks so you finish it to the end.

    - Prioritize schoolwork and your self-care. Remember to sleep and eat well, so you can function well for studying.

    - Reach out for help if you need it, whether it's your family, teachers or others.

    Hotlines for Teens in the US

    - 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Call/Text: 988

    - Crisis Text Line. Text: HOME to 741741

    - Teen Line. Call: 1-800-852-8336 (6pm-10pm PST). Text: TEEN to 839863 (6pm-9pm PST)

    - Boys Town National Hotline. Call: 1-800-448-3000. Text: VOICE to 20121

    - The Trevor Project (for LGBTQ+ teens). Call: 1-866-488-7386. Text: START to 678678

    - NAMI Teen & Young Adult HelpLine. Call: 1-800-950-6264. Text: Friend to 62640
